SECTION 01

Introduction

Business Unit Helsepersonell [health personnel / healthcare professionals] — referred to as Helsepersonell — is a part of Norsk Helsenett SF [Norwegian Health Network, a state enterprise — “SF” = statsforetak], which delivers the national infrastructure for sharing of data and handling of workflow in the Norwegian healthcare sector.

Helsepersonell uses TiddlyWiki for some key management areas:

  • Monthly business review of the different services that Helsepersonell provides.
  • Definition and tracking of OKRs for each service and the business unit.
  • Automation of reporting to the CEO and his team.

Helsepersonell is currently using a vanilla installation of TiddlyWiki, but the quality of the input indicates that the way responsible individuals interact with TiddlyWiki needs to be simplified.

SECTION 02

Structure

The diagram below shows the structure that Helsepersonell maintains in TiddlyWiki.

The governance structure [Styringsstruktur] consists of NHN — this is one tiddler representing the mother company Norsk Helsenett SF. NHN consists of several business units [Forretningsområde — business unit / business area], and each business unit owns and is responsible for a set of services [Tjeneste — service]. These services can be internal or external facing.

Each year the team establishes its intentions [Intensjon — intention] for what it wants to achieve at the different levels in the governance structure. A loose version of OKRs is used to do this: Objectives [Målsetting] and Key Result [Resultat].

The team runs three-week sprints, and each time it delivers it registers a delivery [Leveranse — delivery] in TiddlyWiki and links it to:

  • The OKR structure optional, or
  • The governance structure.

Not included in the diagram: each month there is a business review where each service owner [Tjenesteeier — service owner] writes a small tiddler summarising business change, economics, and a severity flag (1–3) for each service. The business unit goes through these based on severity and changes each month. Today they are linked in the business-unit tiddler.

Today this is all handled using tags in TiddlyWiki.
SECTION 03

Simplification and automation

Based on the experience that Helsepersonell has gained over the last two years using plain TiddlyWiki, there are several needs for simplification and automation. These are summarised in the subsections below.

3.1 Simplified navigation

Make the menu on the right-hand side of TiddlyWiki show a reflection of:

  • The governance structure with the intentions [Intensjon].
  • The business-review structure, separated into year and month.
  • Summarised deliveries [Leveranse] per year and month.
  • Services grouped by business unit [Forretningsområde] and service owner [Tjenesteeier].

3.2 Simplified creation of “correct” tiddlies

Let the user select which type of tiddler they want to create and be guided through the creation, so that tiddlers follow the correct structure.

optional It would also be nice to have this for updating existing tiddlers.

3.3 Export of information from the governance structure and business review, per service

Export information from the governance structure and per-service business review in the format below. Two types of extract are requested.

Extract 1

An Excel extract showing all links (URLs) to tiddlers tagged with one of:

Styring Satsning for fart [Governance: Initiative for speed], Styring Tiltak [Governance: Measure / action], Styring Intern tjeneste [Governance: Internal service], Styring Relatert tjeneste [Governance: Related service], or Styring Ekstern tjeneste [Governance: External service].

The extract should include separate columns showing the fields and tags on the tiddler:

  • ServiceID
  • Division
  • Service name
  • Year
  • Month
  • Business change — Forretningsmessig endring:1 / :2 / :3 [Business change: 1 / 2 / 3]
  • Governance — Styring Satsning for fart, Styring Tiltak, Styring Intern tjeneste, Styring Relatert tjeneste, or Styring Ekstern tjeneste

It must be possible to filter / refine the extract by year and month.

Extract 2

An Excel extract showing all links (URLs) to tiddlers tagged with one of:

Ekstern tjeneste [External service], Intern tjeneste [Internal service], Relatert tjeneste [Related service], Tiltak [Measure / action], or Satsning for fart [Initiative for speed].

The extract should include separate columns showing the fields and tags on the tiddler:

  • ServiceID
  • Division
  • Service name
  • Year
  • Service type — Ekstern tjeneste, Intern tjeneste, Relatert tjeneste, Tiltak, or Satsning for fart

It must be possible to filter / refine the extract by year.

3.4 Summarising of deliveries per year and/or month

Deliveries are used to report to the board and to external entities. The team would like an overview with the following information.

Overview of all tiddlers tagged Leveranse [Delivery], with flexibility to group by:

  • Year (tag) → Month (tag) → Service (Intern tjeneste / Ekstern tjeneste)
  • Service (Intern tjeneste / Ekstern tjeneste) → Year (tag) → Month (tag)

Overview of all tiddlers tagged Målsetting [Objective], with flexibility to group by:

  • Year (tag) → Service (Intern tjeneste / Ekstern tjeneste)
  • Service (Intern tjeneste / Ekstern tjeneste) → Year (tag)

Overview of all tiddlers tagged Resultat [Key result], with flexibility to group by:

  • Year (tag) → Service (Intern tjeneste / Ekstern tjeneste) → Målsetting
  • Service (Intern tjeneste / Ekstern tjeneste) → Year (tag) → Målsetting

3.5 Generation of a ToDo-list for the business review

Generate a ToDo list, per month and per service, identifying where the service owner [Tjenesteeier] has not yet established the business-review tiddler for that month.

This must also check that the tiddler is not just the template, and not just a copy of last month. It should be easy for the service owner to create the necessary tiddler directly from this ToDo list. The service owner can be recorded on the service tiddler.

3.6 Generation of a ToDo-list for OKRs [Intensjon — Intention]

Generate a ToDo list, per year and per service, identifying where the service owner has not yet established the OKRs for that service for the given year.

This must also check that the tiddler is not just the template, and not just a copy of last year’s. It should be easy for the service owner to create the necessary tiddler directly from this ToDo list. The service owner can be recorded on the service tiddler.

3.7 Periodisation and archiving

A solution is needed to periodise the structure per year — and possibly archive the previous year — to keep clutter down.

3.8 Access control optional

The team would like to separate who can work in TiddlyWiki from who can only read. NHN uses Microsoft AD [Active Directory].
